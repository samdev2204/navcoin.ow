NavCoin is an easy to use digital currency - run by its community. It’s programmable money that lowers the cost of doing business in the Web3 economy.

  • Low friction payments

  • Community run since 2014

    Decentralization isn’t just a buzzword. It’s built into the very foundation of the network, to create a culture of permissionless innovation.

NavCoin's Top Features

Carbon Neutral Payments

NavCoin has offset the carbon footprint of the network to become the world’s first carbon neutral cryptocurrency.

Network scalability

Testing shows current capacity at 260+ transactions per second, alongside active research into layer 2 scaling solutions.

Low cost global payments

Send money cheaply, anywhere in the world, without needing to go through a bank.

Privacy

Privacy has always been a central part of NavCoin, from the original NavTech to the upcoming improvements.

Quick payments

Transactions show up in seconds, and are confirmed within 30 seconds, making it ideal for business.

Pay a name, not a number

NavCoin uses OpenAlias to make sending money as easy as typing an email address.

Values That Are Built Into The Network

  • Open & transparent governance

    No one is in control of NavCoin - everyone is free to contribute however they want. There are no forced changes, and every wallet gets a say in the direction of NavCoin.

  • Open source everything

    Open source is the backbone of NavCoin. Every line of code is viewable, and anyone can contribute to the website. Trust has to be built through transparency.

  • Be your own bank

    Being a peer to peer network means that there are no banks or intermediaries getting in the way. An economic system where your vote counts.

  • Building a new system

    NavCoin is symbol of change for how the entire global economic system works - one that returns freedom, privacy, and financial power back to the individual.

  • Fair, clean & green

    No one lives in a bubble - we all affect the world in different ways. This is why NavCoin strives to be energy efficient to do right by our planet.

  • Privacy

    Privacy is about giving you a choice. You get to take back control of your personal information, along with who gets access to it.
